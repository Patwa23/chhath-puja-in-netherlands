Chhath Puja 2025

A Festival of Gratitude to the Sun God (Surya Dev) and Chhathi Maiya

Festival Dates: October 25 to October 28, 2025

Chhath Puja is a four-day ancient Hindu festival primarily celebrated in Bihar, Jharkhand, and Uttar Pradesh.

About Chhath Puja

Chhath Puja is a major Vedic festival dedicated to the **Sun God, Surya**, and **Chhathi Maiya** (a form of Goddess Usha). Devotees, particularly women (known as 'Vrati'), observe a rigorous 36-hour fast (often *Nirjala* - without water) and perform rituals to thank the Sun for sustaining life on Earth and to pray for the well-being, prosperity, and longevity of their families and children. It is unique as it is one of the few Hindu festivals where the setting sun is worshipped along with the rising sun.
